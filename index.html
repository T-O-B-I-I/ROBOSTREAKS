About

It all began with a group of enthusiastic engineer who dreamt of creating and innovating something completely new. We had a great vision, a passion for change and all the right skills. Together, we established Robostreaks, a platform where all like-minded people dedicated to create and innovate could unite. To learn, share ideas and to explore together the world of Robotics and Technology.If you think you are just like us, join us in our enlightening endeavour.
